Michael Chang

Hi! I’m Michael, a machine learning engineer with over two years of experience turning cutting-edge AI ideas into reality. My passion lies in pushing the boundaries of AI through research in reinforcement learning, large language models (LLMs), and multimodal systems.

I’m driven by the dream of contributing as a research scientist at world-renowned AI labs like DeepMind and Meta AI (FAIR), where innovation transforms the future. When I’m not deep in code or research papers, I enjoy volleyball, skiing and playing go.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my projects, publications, and aspirations!
